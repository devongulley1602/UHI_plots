Average Annual Cycles Grouped by Month

The following interactive graphs show the average annual cycles of temperature (in Kelvin) and humidity (as a fraction from 0 to 1) grouped by month to analyse seasonal trends in urban (50% or greater urban fraction) and rural (less than 1% urban fraction) areas. It displays these for when CLASS alone is included in the surface parametrisation, and when CLASS and TEB are both included.
